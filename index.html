About

Our Working Place

Morocco is a surfer’s dream — from the long right-handers of Imsouane to the point breaks of Taghazout. Whether you're chasing winter swells or mellow summer waves, this coastline has it all. Get lost in the rhythm of the tides, the warmth of local hospitality, and the adventure of surfing wild Atlantic waters. At Almarse, we guide you to the best spots, stories, and secrets of surfing in Morocco.
